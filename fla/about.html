Our Education

  • Our Philosphy

    Our goal at Foundation Learning Academy is to establish a community where each child feels happy, safe and secure; and where each adult feels welcomed, respected and valued.

  • Our Mission

    It is within this environment that children develop a capacity for peace, acceptance of individual differences, and a lifelong passion for learning.

  • We care

    We strive to create an environment that values and includes every individual and celebrates unique differences and cultures.

We Strive to:

We aspire to do this so successfully that we make a difference in the lives of children and families and in the communities where we live and work. We strive to meet all the needs to each child – social, emotional, physical and cognitive. Therefore it is our goal to provide a solid foundation for each child’s well-being and education to build upon them as they grow older.

  • Meet all the needs to each child – social, emotional, physical and cognitive
  • Nurture each child’s unique qualities and potential
  • Support families through strong partnerships
  • Create a work environment that encourages professionalism, growth, and diversity
