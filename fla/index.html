Foundation Learning Academy

Our Philosophy

Our goal at Foundation Learning Academy is to establish a community where each child feels happy, safe and secure; and where each adult feels welcomed, respected and valued. It is within this environment that children develop a capacity for peace, acceptance of individual differences, and a lifelong passion for learning

Our Commitment to Diversity

Foundation Learning Academy is a vibrant community made of children, families, and employees who represent many different cultures, backgrounds, and experiences. We strive to create an environment that values and includes every individual and celebrates unique differences and cultures. Our commitment to diversity is fundamental to supporting the success of our company, so that we may understand and respond to the challenges of our clients, the needs of the families whom we serve, and the evolving goals of our employees.
